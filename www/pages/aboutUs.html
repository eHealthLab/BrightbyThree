About Us

  • About Bright by Three

  • Our Mission

    To provide a bright beginning for all Colorado children by helping families support their children’s physical, emotional, and intellectual development during the critical first three years of life.

  • Our Vision

    Every child in Colorado will be healthy, valued, and ready to learn.

  • Our Funding

    We gratefully accept donations from a variety of private foundations, corporations, and individuals including The Colorado Health Foundation, The Daniels Fund, The Denver Foundation, Kaiser Permanente, Amgen, and many others. We don’t currently accept public (governmental) funds.
